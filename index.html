1. about

Iris Hyvönen

Programming student, graphic designer, comic artist, nerd

Oh hi, I almost didn't see you there! Welcome to my humble page, I hope you enjoy your stay.

I am a human mess called Iris, and I study Video Game Programming in Taitotalo, Helsinki. I started in November 2021, and will graduate at the end of 2023, should everything go as expected.

I graduated in Graphic Design in 2019, but that wasn't enough for me. I want to know how things work behind the curtains, how video games are made and programs actually work.

Most recently I've been curious of: tabletop RPGs, JavaScript, Brandon Sanderson's Mistborn and gradient maps!
